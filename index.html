Shrimp Alfredo
#1 Favorite

This great combination is my top favorite because I love fettuccine and also shrimp. Olive Garden has a creamy, homemade alfredo, and it is tossed with sautéed shrimp.

Shrimp Carbonara
#2 Favorite

This is my second favorite because I do not like red peppers, but it was delicious overall. Olive Garden serves it as sautéed shrimp and bucatini pasta tossed in a creamy carbonara sauce with bacon and roasted red peppers.

Chicken And Shrimp Carbonara
#3 Favorite

This was my third favorite because I would have preferred grilled chicken instead of breaded chicken. Olive Garden serves this dish as sautéed seasoned chicken, shrimp, and spaghetti tossed in a creamy sauce with bacon and roasted red peppers.
